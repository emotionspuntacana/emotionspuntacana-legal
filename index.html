About Us

Hi, I'm the founder of EmotionsPuntaCana Tourism Services.

I was born and raised in the Caribbean, Dominican Republic. My native language is Spanish and my second language is English.

As a person, I am very social and enjoy establishing and building relationships. I love meeting people and connecting with every person I meet.

I love the high-tech industry, and that is what I do for a living. However, I recently discovered that my biggest passion and motivation is giving back to others and to the community.

Sometimes we live in our own professional and work environment and forget about the most important things in life: serving God, providing help, offering support, and caring for those who are in real need. For me, that is very important, and it has become part of my life.

At work, I am customer-centric and a team player. I am always open to helping and supporting customers, partners, and team members. I also like to take a fun and enjoyable approach to work. I believe you should love your work and put maximum effort toward the smart goals you want to accomplish.

Regarding my professional career, I have worked as a Telecom, Network, and Architect Engineer for more than 22 years in the design, implementation, configuration, and troubleshooting of IP and Mobility Packet Core Networks.

I have a strong devotion to trending and new technologies, and I spend much of my time around Data Center and Cloud, Routing and Switching, Wireless, Security, NetOps, Programmability, and Service Provider IP/ISP backhaul environments. Most of my work experience comes from enterprise environments and telecom operators, including Orange Dominicana – France Telecom Group, DgTec, T-Mobile, and AT&T USA/Mexico.

My family is my biggest treasure. When I am not busy at work, I spend most of my time with my beautiful wife and kids.
